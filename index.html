Introduction

Adolescent poverty isn't just a temporary hardship; it's a barrier that stifles potential and perpetuates inequality. Empowering our youth with the tools, resources, and support they need is the key to breaking this cycle. To solve this issue, it is important to focus on creating opportunities for education, skill development, and community support, ensuring that no young person is left behind.

Impact

By providing the necessary resources and guidance, we empower the youth of the world to overcome the barriers of poverty, fostering a generation of leaders who can contribute to their communities and break the continuous cycle of disadvantage.
